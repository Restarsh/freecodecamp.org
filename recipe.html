Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Core Feature: Basic chocolate fudge presented in uniform cube shapes.

Presentation: Clean, no-frills plating on a white dish to emphasize the product itself.

No Add-ons Yet: No nuts, sprinkles, or extra ingredients – focusing on pure chocolate flavor.

Form Factor: Stackable, portable cubes ideal for sharing, gifting, or portion control.

Ingredients

  • Sweetened Condensed Milk – 1 can (14 oz)
  • Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips – 3 cups (~18 oz)
  • Butter – 2 tablespoons
  • Vanilla Extract – 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Prepare Your Pan
  2. Melt the Ingredients
  3. Add Vanilla (Optional)
  4. Pour and Spread
  5. Chill
  6. Cut into Squares
